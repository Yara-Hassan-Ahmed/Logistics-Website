About Us

Ocean Blue Shipping Company is a full shipping services office located in Alexandria city - Egypt 10 km away from Alexandria port. We had been engaged for more than 20 years of experience management in chartering, freight forwarding, ship agency, stevedoring, fleet management, and full Warehousing facilities. Ocean Blue Shipping Company is your partner for all types of marine services aiming to meet customers, ship owners, and charterers' demands and new developing marine services. Ocean blue Shipping Company's headquarter is located in Alexandria but in addition we have our own network of supported agency services, stevedoring, and warehousing in all Egyptian ports. We believe that the quality of services given by Ocean blue shipping company achieves all needed job requirements and marine services for clients that are looking for their present and future marine business. Ocean Blue shipping Company is acting also as a shipping general agent in all Egyptian ports for the shipping line "Europe Eastmed Line, Valletta Malta" which is running liner services between Northern Europe to East mediterranean sea and vice versa. Showing a regular liner service on monthly basis for breakbulk cargoes. We are mainly loading out of Antwerp port to Alexandria port - Egypt and Mersin - Turkey or any other intermediate port in direction on the subject of a suitable consignment. We are ready at any time to book your breakbulk cargo whatever its volume and weight are offering full door-to-door picking / delivering services in Europe, Egypt, and Turkey.
