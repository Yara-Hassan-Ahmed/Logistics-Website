Shipping Agency

Ocean Blue shipping is an Egyptian shipping agent authorized in all Egyptian ports serving all types of vessels up to 25000 tons registered tonnage. Major agency services coming for breakbulk, general cargoes vessels carrying all type of goods in/outward Egypt using its long-standing relationship with Turkish and European shipowners to offer agency services for their tonnages such as port entry, provisions supply, and repairing and transit services in Our main port of agency services is in Alexandria port where our headquarter is located in addition we had site jobs at Dekhilla, Abu Qir, and Damietta ports beside Red seaports like Suez "Adabyia and Soukhna ports".
