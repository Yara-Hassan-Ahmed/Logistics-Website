These are the official documents proudly awarded to Ocean Blue Shipping Company.

ISO 9001 Certificate

ISO 9001 is defined as the international standard that specifies requirements for a quality management system (QMS). Organizations use the standard to demonstrate the ability to consistently provide products and services that meet customer and regulatory requirements. It is the most popular standard in the ISO 9000 series and the only standard in the series to which organizations can certify.

Commercial Register

The commercial register is a record or book in which data about a trader is entered, whether he is an individual or a company, and a page is devoted to each trader that includes everything related to his commercial activity and the amendments that occur to it.

Shipping Agency License

The shipping agency license is a vital document to allow any company to perform as a shipping agent to specific types of vessel. This is awarded by the Egyptian Maritime Transport Sector directly governed by the Egyptian Ministry of Transport. This certificates documentates that Ocean Blue Shipping Company can be an agent to vessels having maximum capacity of 25,000 tons.
